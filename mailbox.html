How Mailbox works

Your Mailbox collects coupons and official notices that can help you save money and stay up to date.

  • Coupons: tap to open a Wallet-style pass you can scan at the register.
  • Notices: tap to read the full letter (appointments, reminders, benefit updates).
  • Close: tap outside the pop-up, press the X, or hit Escape.

Tip: keep an eye on expiration dates so you don’t miss deals.
